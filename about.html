Team Members

Emmanuel Misiati Marumbu

Founder & CEO

Certified by the National Industrial Trainers Association, Marumbu is the lead trainer and facilitator of the team. He boasts an array of facilitation and training techniques courtesy of tens of hours of experience. He is also a co-lead of Shiriki, a social engagement program where he acts as an engagement specialist. Additionally, Marumbu is an executive board member of the Taifa Teule Leadership Experience program in charge of facilitator/volunteer development. Among other roles, he is an admin assistant at Maisha Yangu Organization and the Chief Operating Officer at Busara Empowerment Organization.

Alvin Rita Nyaga

Creative Director

Alvin is a community engagement specialist with amazing skills and insights into inter-group relations and dynamics. She is the Program Lead for Shiriki with a lot of experience in program design, team cohesion, and development. She is also an Executive Member of the Taifa Teule Leadership Experience Board and has years of experience in youth training and facilitation. In addition, she is an innovative event curator and a diligent executioner. She has a background in architecture.
